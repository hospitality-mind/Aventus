Nick Patel

Director of Innovation and Strategy

Nick Patel, a distinguished leader in the hospitality industry, serves as the Director of Innovation and Strategy at Aventus Hospitality. As the founder and CEO of 3P Hotels, Nick's entrepreneurial spirit and visionary leadership have propelled him to the forefront of hotel management.

With a strong focus on Choice Hotels, Nick's expertise extends across renowned brands including IHG, Marriott, and Hilton. His hands-on experience in operating and owning 10+ properties within the Choice Hotels portfolio underscores his intimate understanding of industry dynamics and guest expectations.

Nick's extensive network and innovative strategies serve as invaluable assets to Aventus Hospitality. His ability to leverage industry connections and insights enables the company to identify opportunities, streamline operations, and drive revenue growth.

A true trailblazer in the hospitality sector, Nick Patel's visionary approach and wealth of experience are instrumental in shaping Aventus Hospitality's future success. His relentless pursuit of innovation and strategic vision are driving forces behind the company's commitment to excellence and continuous improvement.
